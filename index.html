About Sabrina

Founder of Itzy Bitzy Speech Therapy

Hi! I'm Sabrina Itzhaki — a licensed speech-language pathologist, the heart behind Itzy Bitzy Speech Therapy, and someone who fully believes that learning should feel like fun, not work.

Over the past 5 years, I've worked with hundreds of children across NYC public schools — helping kids speak more clearly, express their ideas, and feel proud of their voices. I specialize in treating speech sound disorders, but I also support children with language delays and early literacy skills — all through personalized, playful, and meaningful sessions.

I'm not your typical therapist.

I believe the best speech sessions are the ones where your child is fully engaged — not because they have to be, but because they want to be. That's why I take a child-led, interest-driven approach: if your child loves superheroes, we're saving the world while practicing sounds. Into baking? We're becoming master play chefs while whipping up some sweet new vocabulary. Let's build speech goals into the things they already love. That's when the magic happens.

Whether your child needs help saying their sounds more clearly or organizing their thoughts into full sentences, I meet them right where they are — and we grow from there, together.

Services

Speech therapy that's built around your child — not the other way around.

At Itzy Bitzy Speech Therapy, every session is designed with one goal in mind: helping your child communicate with more clarity, confidence, and joy.

Whether we're working on saying tricky sounds, building stronger sentences, or boosting early reading skills, we do it in a way that's playful, motivating, and fully tailored to your child's personality and passions.

What I Treat

I specialize in:

Speech Sound Disorders - Trouble saying certain sounds clearly? I help kids with articulation and phonological delays (like lisps, sound substitutions, or unintelligible speech), as well as motor-based speech challenges like childhood apraxia of speech.

I also support:

  • Expressive & Receptive Language Delays - Whether your child is struggling to understand what others say or put their own thoughts into words, I help them build the vocabulary, sentence structure, and confidence they need to express themselves.
  • Literacy & Early Reading Skills - If your child is having trouble with phonemic awareness, decoding, or comprehension, I use research-backed strategies to strengthen the connection between speech and reading.

Other Services

  • Evaluations - I provide comprehensive speech-language evaluations to identify your child's strengths and needs, and create a therapy plan that makes sense for your family.
  • Parent Coaching - You're your child's biggest supporter — and I'll show you easy, everyday ways to keep progress going between sessions. From simple strategies to fun games you can play at home, you'll feel confident knowing exactly how to help.

Session Details

  • Length: 45 minutes or 1 hour — based on your child's needs and your preferences
  • Where: In-home therapy sessions in Brooklyn
  • Frequency: Most families meet 1–2 times per week

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you work with?

I work with children in Pre-K through elementary school — typically ages 3–10. If you're unsure if your child is the right age for services, feel free to reach out!

Where do sessions take place?

I provide home-based speech therapy in Brooklyn, so your child can learn and grow in the comfort of their own space. No rushing, no waiting rooms — just cozy, convenient care right where they feel most at ease.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

At this time, I primarily offer in-person sessions in Brooklyn. If you're interested in teletherapy, let's chat — in some cases, it may be a great option!

How long are sessions?

Sessions are either 45 minutes or 1 hour, depending on your child's needs and what works best for your family.

How much does therapy cost?

Itzy Bitzy Speech Therapy is a private-pay practice, meaning I don't bill insurance directly. However, I'm happy to provide invoices or documentation if you'd like to submit for out-of-network reimbursement through your insurance provider.

Do you offer evaluations?

Yes! I provide comprehensive speech-language evaluations to assess your child's strengths, challenges, and determine whether therapy is recommended. Evaluations include a written report and a follow-up consultation.

What's your approach to therapy?

My approach is child-led, play-based, and interest-driven — because kids learn best when they're having fun. I build each session around what your child enjoys, so therapy feels more like play and less like work. Whether your child loves trains, cooking, or video games, we'll use their interests to motivate and engage them every step of the way.

How do I get started?

Easy! Just send me a quick message or give me a call. I offer free consultations where we'll talk through your concerns, goals, and whether we're a good fit.
