About Sabrina

Founder of Itzy Bitzy Speech Therapy

Hi! I’m Sabrina Itzhaki, a licensed speech-language pathologist, play expert, and proud Brooklyn local.

After five years supporting Pre-K and elementary-aged students across NYC public schools, I created Itzy Bitzy Speech Therapy to bring a fresh, fun, and family-centered approach to speech therapy, one that actually works, because it’s built around what kids love.

I'm not your typical therapist.

At Itzy Bitzy, we don’t just drill sounds... we build confidence, connection, and communication that sticks. Whether your child needs help with clear speech, expressive language, or literacy, I meet them exactly where they are, at home, in their world, and on their terms.

Because here’s the truth: kids learn best when they’re having fun. That’s why my sessions are always:

  • Child-led - no cookie-cutter plans here
  • Play-based - because play is powerful!
  • Tailored to your child's passions - Dinosaurs? Debate team? Minecraft? Let’s go.

    Services

    Speech therapy that's built around your child, not the other way around.

    What I Specialize In

    • Speech Sound Disorders -I help kids speak more clearly and confidently, whether they’re struggling with specific sounds (like lisps or mix-ups) or motor-based challenges like apraxia.
    • Language Delays - From understanding others to expressing thoughts, I support language development with fun, meaningful activities.
    • Literacy & Early Reading - I build the speech-to-reading connection, helping kids with sound awareness, decoding, and comprehension through playful, proven strategies.

    Other Services

    • Evaluations - I offer thoughtful, personalized evaluations to understand your child’s communication needs and create a therapy plan that fits your family.
    • Parent Coaching - You’re a key part of your child’s progress. I’ll give you simple, fun strategies to use at home, so you can support speech and language every day, with confidence.

    Session Details

    • Length: 45 minutes or 1 hour, based on your child's needs and your preferences
    • Where: In-home therapy sessions in Brooklyn
    • Frequency: Most families meet 1–2 times per week
